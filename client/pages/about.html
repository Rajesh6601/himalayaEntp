Our Story

From a Small Workshop to 15,000 sq.ft. Manufacturing Powerhouse

Founded in Jamshedpur, the steel city of India, Himalaya Enterprises has been at the forefront of automobile body manufacturing for over 15 years. What started as a 3-person workshop in Adityapur has grown into one of Eastern India's most respected fabrication companies.

We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern CNC-driven engineering to deliver products that exceed expectations. Our state-of-the-art facility houses cutting-edge equipment for precision cutting, bending, welding, and finishing. Every product goes through a rigorous 12-point quality check before delivery.

Today, we serve fleet owners, transporters, mining companies, and municipal bodies across Jharkhand, Bihar, Odisha, and West Bengal.

About Rajeev Shukla

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

Rajeev Shukla is the visionary behind Himalaya Enterprises. Born and raised in Jamshedpur, he understood the industrial heartbeat of the steel city from a young age. After completing his engineering degree, Rajeev spent several years working in the automobile manufacturing sector before founding Himalaya Enterprises in 2009.

With over two decades of experience, Rajeev has built the company from a small workshop into one of the most respected names in tipper, trailer, and custom fabrication in Eastern India. Under his leadership, the company has manufactured over 500 vehicle bodies and served 200+ clients across Jharkhand, Bihar, Odisha, and West Bengal.

Rajeev's Vision

"My goal has always been simple: build the strongest, most reliable automobile bodies in India. Every tipper, every trailer, every tank that leaves our facility carries our name and our reputation. I want every client to feel that they made the best investment of their career when they chose Himalaya Enterprises."
