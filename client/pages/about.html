Our Story

From a Small Workshop to 95,000 sq.ft. Manufacturing Powerhouse

Founded in Jamshedpur, the steel city of India, Himalaya Enterprises has been at the forefront of automobile body manufacturing for over 22 years. What started as a 3-person workshop in Adityapur has grown into one of Eastern India's most respected fabrication companies.

We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern CNC-driven engineering to deliver products that exceed expectations. Our state-of-the-art facility houses cutting-edge equipment for precision cutting, bending, welding, and finishing. Every product goes through a rigorous 12-point quality check before delivery.

Today, we serve fleet owners, transporters, mining companies, and municipal bodies across Jharkhand, Bihar, Odisha, and West Bengal.

About Rajjeev Shuklaa

Managing Partner

Rajjeev Shuklaa is the visionary behind Himalaya Enterprises. Born and raised in Jamshedpur, he understood the industrial heartbeat of the steel city from a young age. After completing his B.E. in Computer Science from NIT Jamshedpur, MBA from SPJIMR Mumbai, and specialization in Robotics from IIT Delhi, he brought a unique blend of technology and business acumen to the automobile body manufacturing sector, founding the company in 2004. With over two decades of experience, he has built it from a small workshop into one of the most respected names in tipper, trailer, and custom fabrication in Eastern India.

Rajjeev's Vision

"My goal has always been simple: build the strongest, most reliable automobile bodies in India. Every tipper, every trailer, every tank that leaves our facility carries our name and our reputation. I want every client to feel that they made the best investment of their career when they chose Himalaya Enterprises."
